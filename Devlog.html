Devlog

About my Game

I started making a game in August 2024. The idea was to make an story driven RPG with a top-down perspective in pixelart style. But troughout developement my vision and ideas changed and i had to better define the scope of the whole game especially as a single person developer. In this devlog i document the journey of making a game on your own.

January 2026: I started crafting more 3D Models of houses and different kinds of things to make the scene feel more alive. I also fixed and redrew some player sprites, so they are up to date and not missing any animations.

September 2025: I finished a prototype of what the worlds and cities may look like in the future. This Version still needs some polish but overall i feel like it's a huge improvement.

July 2025: After i finished various different 3D Models my vision started to come to life..slowly but surely. I made 3D Models for static objects that wouldn't move, like Houses, Lamps, Tree Trunks; Fences etc...everything that is organic, will be portrayed with 2D sprites, like for example NPC's, Flowers or the Player itself.

July 2025: I realised, i had too little artistic freedom with the 2D top-down perspective, so i made the hard desicion to change the complete style of the old game. I wanted more freedom in portraying the outer world and feel, so i started making 3D Models out of my 2D sprites to achive a 2.5D look.

June 2025: I scrapped the idea of fighting in the overworld. After a lot of testing and different approaches i came to the conclusion that a turn based battle system would fit more...so i drew the characters more detailed so i could better portray them in a turn based battle system. This is the first prototype of how the battle system could look like.

April 2025: The Dialogue System got a small redesign and better character portraits and also the ability to be influenced by player choices.

April 2025: I've drawn and added skilltrees for all party members.

March 2025: I experimented with different mini games i could implement to make the world feel more alive and fun.

February 2025: I gave the whole UI a new look, to make it look more polised and make it easier on the eyes. Also i added a crafting system, so the player would be able to craft new weapons and accesories.

January 2025: I decided to redesign the characters and Tilemap, so it would look more polished.

September 2024: I changed the character selection, so when a charcter is selected, it spawns at the point the Player is now standing. While moving with one Member, the other party members are inactive. Also i added a running state, so the Player could move faster if he wanted to and some fighting Animations.

August 2024: I started making my Game. I build in a simple Inventory System, Dialogue System, Day and NIght Cycle and moveable Player Characters. The Player could switch between all 3 Characters, which made the world feel more alive...But i soon realized that this type of controls could become confusing and Gameplay wise there was no purpose for the player to be able to switch between the 3 positions where the characters were standing, so i decided to change the Way the Player would change from character to character.
