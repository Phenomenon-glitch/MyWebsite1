About my DIY Projects

In my free time i love to do all sorts of creative things..so heres a collection of that..

Recently i came up with an cute Dragon design, so i made a figure of it out of polymer clay.

After i finished the first Dragon figure i was so motivated that i started to make various kechain in that design..

This was my first crochet project ever, at first it was confusing but at some point i got the hang of it.

After the first crochet project turned out pretty decent i made some keychains for my friends. A fish and a octopus.

I made this Gengar out of Polymer clay. After that i painted it with acrylic paint and sprayed it with a glossy finish. it was a gift for a really precious person.

While searching the internet i found out about papercraft. I then stumbled upon this template of a Trueno AE86 and i just had to make it...

This was one of my bigger projects. I made Super Saiyan from Dragon Ball out of polymer clay. Then he was painted with acrilic paint and the black lines were added with markers. It was a christmas gift for a family member.

This was just a litte art project, where i wanted to paint the main characters of the game i am developing right now. I used staedler coloured pencils on an cardboard.

One day i just picked up Live2D because i saw it online and tought it was interesting to work with. Long story short, this is how my persona you see all over my website was born.

Again, i saw something online i had to do. It were these cute chibi characters that were made out of fabric and sewn together. I made one for my best friends birthday, that looked like her.

I found my old shoes in the basement..but i didn't want to throw them away...so i started painting them to my liking. Since my favourite color is green and flames look edgy, these shoes were made.

When i saw cell shaded shoes online i had to try it for myself. I combined it with one of my favourite Games and so these Borderlands-style Shoes were born.

I was a huge Genshin Impact fan so i made this Kazuha figure out of polymer clay, including his sword. He's about 20cm tall and took about 2 full days to make.

Since i love the idea of trading card games but also simple board games, i started to make my own. You can play it with up to 4 people. At the start, everyone randomly gets 4 different monster cards which then can be used to battle against eachother, or capture moremonsters or even evolve them to make them even stronger.
